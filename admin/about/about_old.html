ip4STATICS: interactive problems for STATICS

Use ip4STATICS to build skill and confidence in your STATICS know-how.


What is 'ip4STATICS'?
ip4STATICS is a rich collection of interactive problems for building and verifying your STATICS skills. Both 2d and 3d problems are included.
What do I need to know to use 'ip4STATICS' effectively?
Use ip4STATICS while you are learning the principles and methods of Statics to earn how to apply them. And use ip4STATICS at any time to review and refresh your skills. Use ip4STATICS to assess for yourself how competent you are in Statics problem-solving.
How are the problems organized?
The problems are organized by units which match the most commonly used topics or chapters in popular texts and videos.
Who controls the use of ip4STATICS?
YOU ARE IN COMPLETE CONTROL OF HOW YOU USE ip4STATICS.
(1) You get immediate feedback when you enter an answer. Usually this is a % accuracy measure.
(2) You can ask for an answer at any time.
(3) You can ask for a worked solution at any time.
(4) You can ask for a new set of instance variables, such as loads, lengths, masses, etc. By running multiple instances of a problem you can understand the structure of the problem type, not just how to solve for a particular set of numbers.
